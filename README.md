## Overview

Campus Quest transforms how students engage with university programs.

Instead of passive attendance, students:
- Complete **missions tied to SDGs**
- Submit proof of participation
- Earn **points, badges, and rankings**
- Stay motivated through **real incentives and progression**

## Problem

Universities invest heavily in programs, events, and SDG initiatives — but:

- Students mostly **participate passively**
- There’s **no consistent incentive** to stay engaged
- Engagement doesn’t **compound over time**

---

## Solution

Campus Quest acts as a **connective layer** on top of existing university programs:

- Converts events into **missions**
- Introduces **leaderboards & rewards**
- Tracks engagement for both students and admins
- Aligns everything with **Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)**

---

## Core Features

### Student Side
- Mission dashboard
- Proof submission system
- Points & badge tracking
- Leaderboards (individual + department)
- Mission history & recommendations

### Admin Side
- Create & manage missions
- Verify student submissions
- Monitor campus-wide engagement
- SDG tagging and analytics

### Gamification Layer
- Daily side quests
- Ranking system
- Incentive-driven participation
- Real-world impact tracking

## Business Model

- **University SaaS** (~₱250K/year per institution)
- **Transaction fees** (5–8% on sponsored rewards)
- **Data products** for accreditors and researchers
- **Verifier integrations** (IoT, auditors)
